# Selfcare Snack Reel — SOP

## Overview
Short-form vertical video (15s) showcasing a single selfcare "snack" — an edible beauty hack featuring Maju Black Seed Oil. iPhone/UGC-style footage in a normal kitchen with natural daylight. Must look like a real person filmed it on their phone — NOT a production. Designed for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

**Total duration:** 15 seconds | **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (1080×1920) | **Segments:** 5

---

## Visual Style Bible

- **Setting:** Normal, clean kitchen — light or neutral countertops, everyday appliances visible, lived-in but tidy. NOT a studio set.
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight from a window, soft and even. Normal indoor lighting like a real kitchen — NOT dramatic golden-hour, NOT moody shadows, NOT ring light halos. Think: how a kitchen looks when you film with your iPhone at 11am.
- **Color palette:** Natural, true-to-life colors. Warm but not orange-graded. Skin tones must look realistic, not color-corrected.
- **Camera style:** Shot on iPhone — handheld with subtle natural micro-shake (NOT stabilized, NOT on a gimbal, NOT tripod-smooth). UGC/selfie-style framing. NEVER zoom in or out — all shots are STATIC FRAMING with handheld feel. No dolly, no crane, no slider, no slow zoom. If it looks like a "production," it's wrong.
- **Avatar:** Bree Alba — mixed race woman, late 20s, hair pulled back in a messy bun, wearing a casual black tank top or halter top, minimal/no visible makeup, natural dewy skin. She must look like herself filming a quick kitchen video, NOT like a model on a photoshoot.
- **Product:** Maju's Black Seed Oil 8oz — dark amber glass bottle with cream/gold label reading "BLACK SEED OIL." The bottle must be clearly recognizable and the label readable in at least 2 segments. Show the ACTUAL product, not a generic dark bottle.
- **Props (REQUIRED in every segment):** Maju Black Seed Oil bottle (actual product render), halved red onion, wooden cutting board, pinch of salt
- **Mood:** Casual, authentic, relatable — like a friend showing you a hack in their kitchen. Slightly provocative ("she's really eating a raw onion?!"), confident but NOT polished or overly produced

---

## Formula — 5 Segments

### Segment 1: HOOK — Stop the Scroll (0-3s)

- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Shot:** Medium close-up, Bree Alba holding a whole halved red onion near her face at chin level, looking at it with curiosity then glancing at camera with a confident half-smile. Shot on iPhone, handheld, selfie-style.
- **Framing:** Head and shoulders, onion prominent in lower-third of frame. Static frame — NO zoom.
- **Background:** Normal clean kitchen, Maju Black Seed Oil bottle visible on counter behind her
- **Lighting:** Natural window daylight, soft and even. Normal indoor kitchen lighting.
- **Motion:** Minimal — she raises the onion slightly. Camera is STATIC (handheld micro-shake only, NO zoom, NO dolly).
- **Text overlay:** "de-puff your face snack" OR "wake up puffy? eat this" — bold white text, center-lower third
- **Negative prompt guidance:** No spinning, no 360 rotation, no unnatural head movements, no morphing

### Segment 2: THE REVEAL — Ingredients + Pour (3-6s)

- **Duration:** 3 seconds
- **Shot:** Slightly wider (waist-up), Bree Alba pouring black seed oil from the Maju bottle onto a halved red onion on a wooden cutting board. Shot on iPhone, handheld.
- **Framing:** Bottle label "BLACK SEED OIL" must face camera and be readable. Hands steady. Static frame — NO zoom.
- **Background:** Same normal kitchen, counter with cutting board
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight, overhead kitchen light. Oil should glisten naturally.
- **Motion:** Smooth downward pour only. Camera is STATIC (handheld micro-shake only, NO zoom).
- **Text overlay:** "1 red onion\n+ black seed oil\n+ salt" — clean white text, left-aligned, lower third
- **Negative prompt guidance:** No spinning, bottle stays upright, no jerky movements

### Segment 3: THE DEMO — Eating the Snack (6-11s)

- **Duration:** 5 seconds
- **Shot:** Tight close-up, Bree Alba's face from nose to chin, bringing the oiled onion half to her mouth and taking a big bite. Shot on iPhone, close-up handheld.
- **Framing:** Close-up on the bite — lips, onion, teeth. Raw and authentic. Static frame — NO zoom.
- **Background:** Blurred normal kitchen, Maju bottle soft-focus behind her
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight, even. Oil glistens naturally on onion surface.
- **Motion:** Approach of onion to mouth, decisive bite, chewing. Camera is STATIC (handheld micro-shake only, NO zoom).
- **Reaction:** Must be AUTHENTIC — slight wince at the raw onion taste, then acceptance/confidence. NOT polished or fake-smiling
- **Text overlay:** NONE — let the visual shock do the work
- **Negative prompt guidance:** No spinning, natural facial expressions only, no distorted features

### Segment 4: RESULT + BENEFITS — Educate (11-13s)

- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Shot:** Medium close-up, Bree Alba holding the bitten onion near her face at cheek level, free hand gently touching her cheekbone. Shot on iPhone, handheld.
- **Framing:** Face centered, onion in one hand, other hand on face. Static frame — NO zoom.
- **Background:** Normal kitchen, Maju bottle prominently visible on counter
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight, soft and flattering.
- **Motion:** Gentle touch of cheek, calm expression, eye contact with camera. Camera is STATIC (NO zoom).
- **Text overlay:** "anti-inflammatory\nreduces fine lines" OR "drains facial bloat\nreduces water retention\ntightens puffy skin" — white text, center
- **Negative prompt guidance:** No spinning, minimal movement, poised and calm

### Segment 5: THE GLOW — CTA + Payoff (13-15s)

- **Duration:** 2 seconds
- **Shot:** Medium close-up, Bree Alba's face with both hands framing/touching her face, serene expression, dewy skin. Shot on iPhone, handheld.
- **Framing:** Face fills frame, Maju Black Seed Oil bottle placed prominently nearby. Static frame — NO zoom.
- **Background:** Normal clean kitchen setting
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight, soft. Skin looks healthy and natural — NOT over-lit or glowy.
- **Motion:** Gentle face touch with both hands. Camera is STATIC (handheld micro-shake only, NO zoom). She looks directly at camera.
- **Text overlay:** "anti wrinkle snack\n(onion + black seed oil + salt)" + CTA "link in bio" OR "shop now"
- **Negative prompt guidance:** No spinning, serene slow motion only

## Production Notes

- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 vertical (1080×1920 minimum)
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight from window, normal indoor kitchen lighting. NEVER dramatic golden-hour, NEVER ring light halos, NEVER fluorescent or cool/blue tones. It should look like a normal person filming in their kitchen.
- **Camera feel:** iPhone/UGC style — handheld micro-shake, NOT stabilized or gimbal-smooth. ABSOLUTELY NO ZOOM of any kind (no slow zoom, no dolly zoom, no push-in, no pull-out). Static framing only.
- **Music:** Lo-fi ambient, ASMR-style subtle beats, or trending audio. Casual kitchen vibe.
- **Captions:** Always include — 85% of social video is watched on mute. White bold text, black outline for readability
- **Versions:** Generate 3 versions minimum for A/B testing
- **A/B test variables:** Hook text variants, CTA text ("save for later" / "link in bio" / "shop now"), background music style, segment pacing

## AI Video Generation Rules

- **Image model:** Flux Kontext Max — best photorealistic quality, natural skin, high detail
- **Video model:** Kling 2.6 (`kling-v2-6`) — pro mode for quality, supports native audio
- **Negative prompt (ALL segments):** "spinning, rotating, 360 turn, slow zoom, zoom in, zoom out, dolly zoom, push in, pull out, crane shot, slider, gimbal, stabilized, unnatural motion, distorted face, blurry, low quality, morphing, glitch, cartoon, anime, overexposed, bright white kitchen, fluorescent lighting, dramatic lighting, golden hour, ring light, studio lighting, cinematic color grade"
- **enhance_prompt:** DISABLED — prompts are pre-optimized, AI rewriting causes drift
- **End-frame constraint:** Use when available to anchor the final pose of each segment
- **Camera motion rule:** ALL segments use STATIC framing (no camera movement). The only motion is from the subject (Bree) and props. Camera has natural handheld micro-shake like an iPhone held in hand.
- **Per-segment subject motion notes:**
  - Hook: slight raise of onion, minimal movement
  - Reveal: smooth pour only, hands move, camera stays still
  - Demo: approach + bite, natural chewing
  - Result: nearly still, gentle touch
  - Glow: slow face touch, serene
