# Selfcare Snack Reel — SOP

## Overview
Short-form vertical video (15-30s) showcasing a single selfcare product in a quick, satisfying, ASMR-style format. Designed for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

## Formula

### 1. Hook (0-3s)
- **Visual:** Close-up of the product with dramatic lighting or satisfying texture shot
- **Audio:** ASMR sound or trending audio clip
- **Text overlay:** Bold question or statement ("Your skin is begging for this…")

### 2. The Reveal (3-8s)
- Show the product name and key benefit
- Quick cut to the product being used (squeezing, applying, swatching)
- Keep motion smooth and satisfying

### 3. Application / Demo (8-18s)
- Show real application on skin/hair/body
- Use close-ups to highlight texture, absorption, glow
- Optional: before/after split screen

### 4. Result + CTA (18-25s)
- Final "glow" shot or satisfied reaction
- Text overlay with product name + price or discount code
- CTA: "Link in bio" / "Shop now" / "Save for later"

### 5. End Card (25-30s)
- Brand logo + tagline
- Optional: "Follow for more selfcare snacks"

## Production Notes
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 vertical
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920 minimum
- **Lighting:** Soft, warm, flattering — ring light or golden hour
- **Music:** Trending audio or royalty-free lo-fi/ASMR
- **Captions:** Always include — 85% of social video is watched on mute
- **Versions:** Generate 3 versions minimum for A/B testing

## AI Avatar Guidelines
- Avatar should look natural and relatable
- Skin tone and features should match target demographic
- Expressions: calm, confident, glowing
- Wardrobe: minimal, clean, neutral tones
