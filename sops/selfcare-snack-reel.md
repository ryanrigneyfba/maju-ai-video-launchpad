## 1. Overview

This SOP defines exactly how to produce a "Selfcare Snack Reel" featuring red onion + Maju Black Seed Oil, modeled after the viral format popularized by @selina.dasilvaaaa (28.3K likes, 186 comments) but repositioned around the anti-puffy face angle. Puffiness is one of the top skin pain points — it's visible, hard to hide, and people wake up with it every morning. The video is a short-form vertical reel (15-30 seconds) designed for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

The formula is simple: a person shows raw ingredients with skin benefits, prepares a quick "snack," eats it on camera with an authentic reaction, then shows the "glow" result — all while the product (Maju Black Seed Oil) stays prominently visible throughout.

## 2. Shot-by-Shot Breakdown (Frame-by-Frame)

This is the exact sequence the tool must replicate. Each segment maps to a Higgsfield generation prompt. The timings below are based on analysis of both the reference Instagram video and the existing Maju attempt (selfcare_snack_final.mov).

### Segment 1: HOOK (0-3s)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll. Create instant curiosity about what the person is holding and why.

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| Camera | Medium close-up: avatar from chest up, facing camera directly |
| Avatar Action | Holds a whole red onion up near face with one hand, examining it with a curious/confident expression. Eyes looking at onion then at camera. |
| Product Placement | Maju Black Seed Oil 8oz bottle MUST be visible on the counter/table beside avatar. Label facing camera. Do NOT hold the bottle yet. |
| Lighting | Warm, golden-hour kitchen lighting. Soft shadows. Natural window light from one side preferred. |
| Background | Kitchen setting — dark/moody tones (dark cabinets, warm wood). NOT a sterile white kitchen. |
| Text Overlay | "de-puff your face snack" OR "wake up puffy? eat this" — white text, center-bottom, subtle fade-in at ~1s |
| Audio | Trending ASMR/lo-fi audio clip OR satisfying cutting/prep sound |
| Motion | Slow, deliberate. Avatar slightly tilts head, lifts onion toward camera. Smooth, not jerky. |

**Higgsfield Prompt:** "Medium close-up of a woman in a dark kitchen holding a whole red onion near her face, looking at it curiously then at camera with a confident smile. A bottle of black seed oil sits on the wooden counter beside her. Warm golden lighting from window. She slowly raises the onion. 9:16 vertical, 3 seconds, smooth motion."

### Segment 2: THE REVEAL — Ingredients + Pour (3-6s)

**Purpose:** Show exactly what the "snack" is. Product name and key ingredients become visible. This is the money shot for product placement.

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| Camera | Slightly wider shot: avatar from waist up, looking down at cutting board |
| Avatar Action | Red onion is now halved on a wooden cutting board. Avatar picks up Maju Black Seed Oil bottle and POURS oil directly onto the cut face of the onion half. Slow, satisfying pour. |
| Product Placement | CRITICAL: Maju bottle label MUST face camera during pour. The bottle is the star of this segment. Viewer must be able to read "BLACK SEED OIL" on the label. |
| Props | Wooden cutting board, halved red onion (cut side up showing rings), pinch of salt visible on the onion |
| Text Overlay | "1 red onion\n+ black seed oil\n+ salt" — white text, stacked vertically, center of frame. Each ingredient on its own line with a "+" prefix. |
| Audio | Oil pouring sound (satisfying liquid ASMR). Continue trending audio underneath. |
| Motion | Smooth tilt of the bottle, oil streams visibly onto onion. Keep it slow and satisfying — this is the ASMR moment. |

**Higgsfield Prompt:** "Woman pouring black seed oil from a dark bottle onto a halved red onion on a wooden cutting board. Camera slightly wider, waist up. She looks down at the onion as she pours. The bottle label reading BLACK SEED OIL faces the camera. Warm kitchen lighting, dark moody background. Smooth satisfying pour motion. 9:16 vertical, 3 seconds."

### Segment 3: THE DEMO — Eating the Snack (6-11s)

**Purpose:** The avatar actually eats the raw onion on camera. This is the viral hook — the authentic, slightly uncomfortable reaction is what people share and comment on.

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| Camera | Tight close-up: face and hand with onion, filling most of frame |
| Avatar Action | BITE 1 (6-8s): Avatar lifts the oiled onion half to mouth and takes a big bite. Eyes slightly wide, committing to it. BITE 2 (8-9s): Chewing. Slight grimace/scrunch of nose — but keeps eating. Authentic reaction. COMPOSURE (9-11s): Settles into it. Chews more calmly, maybe nods slightly like "okay this works." |
| Product Placement | Maju bottle MUST remain visible in background throughout (on counter or shelf behind avatar). Never let it leave frame. |
| Expression Key | This is NOT a polished beauty shot. The power is in the RAW AUTHENTICITY. Avatar should look real — slight discomfort, then acceptance. This is what drives comments and shares. |
| Text Overlay | NONE during eating (let the visual do the work). Text returns at the end of this segment. |
| Audio | Crunching/biting ASMR sound. Continue background audio. The crunch is satisfying and attention-holding. |

**Higgsfield Prompt:** "Tight close-up of woman biting into a raw red onion half glistening with oil. She takes a big bite, chews with a slight grimace then settles into it and nods. A bottle of black seed oil is visible on the counter behind her. Warm golden kitchen lighting. Authentic, unpolished reaction. 9:16 vertical, 5 seconds, natural motion."

### Segment 4: RESULT + BENEFITS (11-13s)

**Purpose:** Educate the viewer on WHY this works. Stack the skin benefits to justify the uncomfortable eating moment they just watched.

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| Camera | Medium close-up: avatar holding remaining onion piece near face, looking confident at camera |
| Avatar Action | Holds the bitten onion casually near her chin/cheek. Calm, knowing expression. Maybe touches her face with free hand to gesture at skin quality. |
| Product Placement | Maju bottle still visible in frame (background or held in other hand) |
| Text Overlay | Three benefit lines stacked, appearing one by one: "drains facial bloat" / "reduces water retention" / "tightens puffy skin" — White text, left-aligned, center of frame. Each line fades in 0.5s apart. |
| Audio | Continue trending audio. Soft, confident energy. |

**Higgsfield Prompt:** "Woman holding a bitten red onion near her face, looking confidently at camera. She gently touches her cheek with her free hand. Black seed oil bottle visible on counter. Warm golden lighting. Calm, satisfied expression. 9:16 vertical, 2 seconds."

### Segment 5: THE GLOW — Result + CTA (13-15s)

**Purpose:** The payoff. Show glowing skin as proof the snack "works." Reinforce the product and give the viewer a reason to buy.

| Element | Specification |
|---|---|
| Camera | DIFFERENT ANGLE: Mirror selfie shot OR slightly different framing to signal a "time jump" / result moment |
| Avatar Action | Avatar touches/strokes face gently, looking at herself in mirror or at camera. Expression: satisfied, glowing, serene. Head slightly tilted. This is the "after" beauty shot. |
| Product Placement | Maju bottle prominently placed in foreground or held up near face. THIS IS THE FINAL PRODUCT IMPRESSION — make it count. |
| Skin Appearance | Dewy, glowing, healthy. Warm light hitting the face to enhance the "glow" effect. This is aspirational. |
| Text Overlay | Line 1: "anti-puffy face snack" (larger, bold) / Line 2: "(onion + black seed oil + salt)" (smaller, parenthetical) — White text, center frame. |
| CTA Option | "Link in bio" / "Save for later" / "Shop now" — small text at very bottom of frame |
| Audio | Audio fades out or hits a satisfying resolution beat |

**Higgsfield Prompt:** "Woman looking at herself in a mirror, gently touching her glowing face with both hands. Dewy, healthy skin. She looks serene and satisfied. A bottle of black seed oil is prominently placed in the foreground near the mirror. Warm, soft lighting emphasizes skin glow. 9:16 vertical, 2 seconds, slow smooth motion."

## 9. Quality Checklist (Before Approval)

Every generated video MUST pass all of these checks before being approved in the queue:

- [ ] Maju Black Seed Oil bottle is visible in EVERY segment (never leaves frame)
- [ ] Bottle label is readable in at least 2 segments (Reveal + Glow minimum)
- [ ] Red onion is clearly identifiable (not ambiguous purple blob)
- [ ] Oil pour is visible and satisfying (not too fast, not too slow)
- [ ] Eating reaction looks AUTHENTIC (slight grimace, not overly polished)
- [ ] Glow shot shows dewy, healthy-looking skin
- [ ] All text overlays are readable and properly timed
- [ ] Captions are auto-generated and present
- [ ] Video is 9:16 vertical, 15-20 seconds, 30fps
- [ ] Transitions between segments are smooth (no jarring cuts or black frames)
- [ ] Avatar movement is natural, not robotic or AI-stiff
- [ ] Audio levels are normalized (no sudden volume changes between segments)
